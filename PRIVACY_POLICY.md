# Privacy Policy for Life Organizer

**Effective Date: April 29, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how "Life Organizer" ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Audio Data (Microphone)
Our app requires the `RECORD_AUDIO` permission to provide speech-to-text functionality (e.g., for creating tasks or notes via voice). 
*   **Processing:** Audio is processed locally on your device or via standard system services provided by your operating system (Android/iOS).
*   **Storage:** We do **not** store or record your audio on our servers. The resulting text is stored locally on your device or in your personal database.

### Personal Data
"Life Organizer" may store tasks, notes, and schedules that you create. This data is stored:
*   Locally on your device.
*   In your private Supabase database (if configured).

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the features of "Life Organizer", specifically:
*   To convert your voice into text for easy data entry.
*   To organize and display your life planning data.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do **not** sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or audio data to third parties.

## 4. Data Security
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 5. Your Consent
By using our application, you consent to our Privacy Policy.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us via our GitHub repository.
